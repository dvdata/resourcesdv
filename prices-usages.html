Dark Valley offers three simple royalty free music options

Pay once and use the music in almost any type of audio/visual production, as many times as you wish (unlimited needledrops), for as long as you like (in perpetuity).

  • 1

    Standard use

    Default use. Designed for medium-sized projects. Covers a lot of popular types of use, worldwide. All prices on the website represent the cost of the standard use.

  • 2

    Extended use

    More advanced use, suitable for big-sized projects. This use extends the standard use with many unlimited options and adds wider broadcast use.

  • 3

    Premium use

    The most advanced use for huge projects. This use adds almost unlimited use of music in TV projects, major films and commercial theatrical releases worldwide.

  • The usages are upgradeable. You can start with one usage and then upgrade to more advanced usage if necessary.
  • All usages allow you to use the music solely in your own projects. However, if you are working on a client's project, you can purchase a usage for and on behalf of your clients.

Prohibited Uses
You may not:
Sell, trade in, give away or distribute the music itself;
